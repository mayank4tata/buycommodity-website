About Us

Built on Trust.
Designed for Collaboration.

Buy Commodity is a technology-enabled trade platform built to bring buyers, sellers, financiers, logistics partners and service providers into one connected transaction ecosystem.

We are creating a more transparent, efficient and dependable way for businesses to source, supply, finance and move various commodities across markets.

Backed by over 60 years of cumulative experience across steel manufacturing, supply chain operations, corporate banking and industrial procurement, we understand the importance of reliable supply, fair pricing, transparent execution and trusted partners.

Our platform reduces friction across the transaction lifecycle — from product discovery and pricing to credit evaluation, logistics coordination, documentation and transaction support.

Whether sourcing material, supplying to customers, arranging finance or coordinating movement, Buy Commodity brings every stakeholder onto one platform with greater visibility, confidence and control.
